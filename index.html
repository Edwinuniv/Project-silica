Project Silica: Preserving Data for 10,000 Years

Revolutionary glass-based data storage technology from Microsoft Research

What is Project Silica?

Project Silica is a groundbreaking research project from Microsoft aimed at developing long-term, high-density data storage using quartz glass. This revolutionary approach could transform how we preserve humanity's most valuable information.

Think of it as a modern, incredibly advanced version of etching information onto stone tablets, but using femtosecond lasers and AI in a tiny piece of glass.Unlike traditional storage methods that degrade over time, Project Silica offers unprecedented durability and longevity.

💾 Digital data preservation

Why This Technology Matters

In our digital age, we're generating more data than ever before, but current storage solutions have significant limitations. Project Silica addresses these challenges with a completely new approach to data preservation that could last for millennia.
